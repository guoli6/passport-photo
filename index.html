Chinese Passport Photo Requirements

  • Size: 33mm x 48mm
  • Background: White, uniform, no shadows
  • Head height: 28 - 33mm
  • Top margin: 3 - 5mm above the head
  • Expression: Neutral, mouth closed, both eyes open
  • NOT allowed: Hats, glasses, face coverings, head tilted
  • Face: Centered, looking straight at camera, ears visible

Canadian PR Photo Requirements

  • Size: 50mm x 70mm (2" x 2¾")
  • Background: White or light colored
  • Face height: 31 - 36mm (chin to crown)
  • Expression: Neutral, eyes open, mouth closed
  • NOT allowed: Hats, sunglasses, face coverings
  • Face: Centered, full front view, head and shoulders
  • Photo: Taken by commercial photographer within 12 months
